Learn With Me

Learn with Me is a collection of structured guides on resources that I've used to learn some of my favorite topics. For example, the the Deep Learnin Book by Ian Goodfellow et.al has been a godsend for learning, in-depth, the mathematical foundations of neural networks. However, it's a bit dense for people who aren't exceptional at undergrad-level math.
[To remedy this (1)] I decided to keep a detailed set of notes that explains each concept in more simple terms. This means the study guide is a little more verbose, but it definitely helps when you find yourself stuck on a particular example. I've also made it possible to suggest edits to the page, and [other things as well(2)].
